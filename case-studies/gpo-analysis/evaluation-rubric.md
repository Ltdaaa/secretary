# gpo-analysis Evaluation Rubric

## Judge-Facing Scorecard

| Dimension | Weight | Champion-Level Evidence |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Company realism | 15 | Executive, product, market, technology, operations, finance, legal, and AI teams all have artifacts |
| Multi-agent collaboration | 15 | Clear roles, handoffs, artifacts, and conflict resolution |
| Technical depth | 20 | Data, model, training, fine-tuning, testing, MLOps, and safety all present |
| Product value | 15 | PRD, user research, workflow, adoption path, and customer success loop |
| Business model | 10 | Cost, ROI assumptions, launch motion, and growth metrics |
| Stability | 10 | CPU-first demo works without external services |
| Evaluation rigor | 10 | Metrics, red-team cases, thresholds, regression plan |
| Presentation | 5 | 3-minute demo and technical walkthrough are coherent |

## Release Gate

The project is not ready for submission until:

- A public demo or local reproducible demo exists.
- The output package contains all numbered company artifacts.
- The model route includes baseline before fine-tuning.
- Technology engineering, MLOps, safety, legal, finance, operations, and customer success are represented.
- The judge summary explains what is real, what is simulated, and what is future work.
