## Operating Cadence

1. CEO sets strategic decision and success metric.
2. Product and market teams validate user value.
3. AI platform teams prove data, model, training, evaluation, and release readiness.
4. Legal, safety, finance, operations, and customer success approve launch posture.
5. Delivery team turns evidence into demo, dashboard, and submission story.
